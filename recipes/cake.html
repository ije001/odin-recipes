Cake Recipe

Vanilla Cake

This is my best vanilla cake recipe. A classic butter cake but with Japanese techniques applied for the most plush, soft and moist yellow cake like you have never had before.

Ingredients

  • Plain flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vailla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar for a whole 7 minutes until thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to white, and tripled in volume (just over double depth in the bowl).
  2. Whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between. This should make the flour just incorporated .
  3. use a microwave or stove to heat milk with the butter until it melts. Believe it or not, HOT milk helps with aeration in this cake..
  4. Mix some batter into hot milk – this serves two purposes: a bring down the temperature of the hot milk so it wont “cook” the eggs (ie. tempering); and b it lightens up the density of the hot milk
