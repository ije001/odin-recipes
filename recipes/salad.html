Salad Recipe

Home-made Salad

The classic Nigerian Salad recipe is a very lively salad. It is colorful, nutrient-dense, very filling and it is very tasty

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cabbage
  • Tomato
  • pasta
  • Eggs
  • Cucumber
  • Baked Beans
  • sweet corn

Steps

  1. pull the lettuce leaves apart, and wash and dry them well. To avoid bruising or tearing the leaves, I recommend using a salad spinner
  2. If you do not have a salad spinner, spread the washed lettuce on a kitchen towel to dry while you prepare the other salad components.
  3. After I prep my greens, I finish this recipe with thinly sliced cucumbers, avocado, shaved Parmesan, and savory roasted nuts.
